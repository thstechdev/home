Welcome to TechDev

Who are we?
We are an app-development club where students can have an opportunity to learn and cooperate with others in creating interactive applications and software. We meet every week on Wednesdays in room 325.
Please feel free to browse our website and join us!

At TechDev, we have many competitions you can compete in with your design and coding skills.

We have a curriculum focused primarily on programming with different app-inventing platforms such as Unity and languages such as Swift, Java, and C++. As the year progresses, members will be exposed to hands-on learning environments where they will begin to create their own applications, which will eventually be entered into competitions.
