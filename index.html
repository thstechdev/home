Welcome to TechDev

Who are we?
We are an technology-development club where students can have an opportunity to learn and cooperate with others in creating interactive applications and software. We meet every week on Wednesdays in room 325.
Please feel free to browse our website and join us!

Our Branches


Choose a branch, if you haven't already! FORM HERE

Web Development

A branch focused on both the backend and frontend of web applications. Languages may include HTML, CSS, Javascript (frontend) and Ruby, SQL, Python (backend).

Application/Practical Programming

A branch focused on the development of general applications, stressing the student's ability to access and use existing codebases and other materials for their work. Applications may be mobile and desktop-based, depending on the user. Languages may include Python, Java, Swift, etc.

Graphical Development

A branch focused on the development of graphical applications. Students will develop an understanding of graphical APIs and their interactions. This includes manipulating images and sounds, for example creating the hard code behind a video game.
