So, what's a hackathon?

It's not all about code, and you don't need to be super techy/nerdy to join.

p.s., it's not even about hacking.
A hackathon is a competitive event where people of different skillsets and backgrounds come together and figure out new solutions to problems. It's a way for companies to generate new ideas, for individuals to create new startups, for colleges to generate creativity on their campus. Read more about hackathons here.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join?

ANYONE! We are not limiting our event to just Troy High School students, but all students in Orange County!
THIS HACKATHON calls for a diverse group of students. We are calling business, design, engineering, and coding students. Everyone who comes will be able to utilize their skillsets to the fullest. If you aren't too sure, that's okay too! We want you to come, learn, work in a team, and most of all, have fun!

Why should I join the event?

A hackathon is aimed to spur new ideas in a community- your team might think up the next big thing, who knows? You get to learn new skills, talk to new amazing people, and have fun. Plus, we have prizes, where the top three teams are able to get over $20 each in prizes. AND, DID I MENTION FREE FOOD? I'd say thats a deal!






How are teams made? (What if I don't have any friends?)

NEVER FEAR! At the beginning of the hackathon, we will give you guys time to talk, mingle, and join teams. You will be wearing badges to designate if you are more of a businessperson, designer, engineer, or programmer, so every team has a good balance. Don't worry, the Techdev staff are here to help and will make sure you have an amazing time!

What projects are we supposed to make?

The hackathon will release a theme the day of. Projects must be relevant to the theme, and can be anything from a Java program or a website to a design file or an app/game. Use your creativity!

For example: if the theme was "food", teams can make projects tackling shortening lines in a restaurant to world hunger. Up to you!




What do I need to bring?

We suggest you bring a laptop and a charger to make collaboration easier with your peers, as well as comfortable clothing, music, and earphones. But, above all, bring your creativity!

WHAT ARE THE PRIZES?!?!?!

IF YOU SIGN UP BEFORE 10/10, you get free shirts, free stickers, AND free food. THAT'S A DEAL. If you place as one of the top three teams, you can get up to $25 INDIVIDUALLY. SO SIGN UP.
